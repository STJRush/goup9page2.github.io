The Tesla Model 3

What is the Tesla Model 3?

The Tesla Model 3 is the newest Model to the Tesla collection and is a smaller, simpler, more affordable electric car.

The standard car starts at $35k, will do 220 miles on a charge, hit 60mph in 5.6 seconds and top out at 130mph. The Long Range version which starts at $44k, is good for 310 miles, 0-60mph comes up in 5.1 seconds, and it has a 140mph top speed.

To get the Model 3 you must reserve it 12-18 months before and must pay $1,000 in advance

The car will go up to 310 miles before it needs a charge.
